Strawberry Milkshake

Description:

A milkshake makes everyone happy!

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Strawberries
  • Ice cubes
  • Ice cream

Steps:

  1. Pour milk, ice cream, ice cubes, and strawberries into a blender
  2. Blend until mixed well
  3. Pour into glass
  4. Drink this comfortang milkshake!
