Pepperoni Pizza

Description:

Hot and fresh cheesy pepperoni pizza with a charred crust.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza crust
  • Pizza suace
  • Shredded cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps:

  1. Spread pizza sauce onto the crust
  2. Sprinkle cheese onto the sauce
  3. Place pepperonis
  4. Bake pizza in over for 8-15 mins at about 450F
  5. Time to enjoy!
